abstract: 'This paper presents the construction of an Advanced Traveller Information System (ATIS)
for the public transport system of Ciudad Universitaria which is the main campus of the
Universidad Nacional Autónoma de México. The CU public transport system consists of
buses (free for all users), a bike-share system (free only for the campus community) and
pedestrians. The aim of the presented ATIS is to find shortest hyperpaths where the buses
are frequency-based, but for a period of time, there is real-time information on bus arrivals.
An outline of the hypergraph model and the algorithm for finding solutions is described to
get a panorama of the transport problem in CU, although it is not the main purpose of the
paper to fully describe the algorithm and the model.'
